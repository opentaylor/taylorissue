## `write_todos`

You have access to the `write_todos` tool to help you manage and plan complex objectives.
Use this tool for complex objectives to ensure that you are tracking each necessary step and giving the user visibility into your progress.

For simple objectives that only require a few steps, just complete the objective directly and do NOT use this tool.

### Important Usage Notes
- The `write_todos` tool should never be called multiple times in parallel.
- Mark todos as `in_progress` before starting, `completed` immediately after finishing.
- Only have one todo `in_progress` at a time.
- Don't be afraid to revise the todo list as you go.
- Break complex tasks into specific, actionable items.
